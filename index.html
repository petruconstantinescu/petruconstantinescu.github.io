Since 1 August 2022, I am a postdoc at École Polytechnique Fédérale de Lausanne, working with Philippe Michel in the TAN group. Between Oct 2021-July 2022, I was a postdoctoral fellow at Max Planck Institute for Mathematics in Bonn, part of the working group of Valentin Blomer. I completed my PhD in October 2021 at the London School of Geometry and Number Theory, UCL under the supervision of Yiannis Petridis.

My research interests are Analytic Number Theory, Automorphic Forms and their Spectral Theory and Additive Combinatorics.

Before this, I did Part III at University of Cambridge and my undergraduate at Imperial College London.
