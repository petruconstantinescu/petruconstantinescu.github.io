I am a postdoctoral fellow at the Max Planck Institute for Mathematics in Bonn. My mentor is Valentin Blomer. Previously, I completed my PhD at the London School of Geometry and Number Theory, UCL under the supervision of Yiannis Petridis.

My research interests are Analytic Number Theory, Automorphic Forms and their Spectral Theory and Additive Combinatorics.

Before this, I did Part III at University of Cambridge and my undergraduate at Imperial College London.
